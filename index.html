FAQ

What do you need to quote? Device type, pack voltage, clear photos, symptoms, and whether it was stored for a long time.
Can you increase capacity? Sometimes — depends on physical space, current requirements, and cell availability. Ask for options.
Do you repair water/fire damaged packs? Usually no. For safety and reliability I may refuse packs with severe damage.
How long does it take? Depends on the pack and parts availability. I’ll confirm after diagnostics.

Disclaimer: Repairs involve used equipment and varying pack history. I test and verify, but no repair can guarantee original manufacturer performance.
